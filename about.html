About Us

At Reflect Collective, our mission is simple yet powerful: to end sexual and domestic violence for everyone.


Reflect Collective is a 501c3 non-profit led by queer, trans people of color who are survivors of violence. We recognize that QTPOC often face the highest levels of violence due to stigma, discrimination, and systemic oppression. That’s why our work is rooted in uplifting and supporting these communities first. We believe that true justice comes through collective healing because liberation is only possible when the most marginalized are free.

To bring this vision to life, we:

  • Community healing events that center survivors and their recovery.
  • Provide direct financial assistance to QTPOC survivors.
  • Educate youth on healthy relationships, consent, and respect.

Our goal is to not only address the epidemic of sexual and domestic violence in QTPOC communities, but to build a safer, more compassionate South Florida for everyone.
